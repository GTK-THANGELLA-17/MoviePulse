Welcome to Opinion and Interest Poll

Our platform aims to gather and analyze movie genre preferences from audiences around the world. This information helps filmmakers and directors understand what types of movies are popular in different regions and industries.

Why Participate?

Your opinion matters! By participating in this poll, you contribute valuable insights into the movie industry, helping creators tailor their films to better match audience preferences.

What is this Poll About?

Every six months, we collect data on genre preferences in various film industries, including Tollywood, Bollywood, and Hollywood. This data is then presented in a comprehensive manner, showing trends and patterns that can influence future film projects.

How Does it Work?

  • Select your country.
  • Based on your country, choose the relevant film industry.
  • Vote for your favorite genres.
  • See the aggregated results and trends.
